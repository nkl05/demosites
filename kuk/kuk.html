ABOUT US

The Kurukshetra University was established in 1956 as a utility residential University and its foundation stone was laid by late Dr.Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India. Located in the holy city of kurukshetra, land of historical battle of 'Mahabharata' and the great message of Bhagwata Gita, its campus is situated on the western bank of Brahm Sarover(the holy tabk) and extends over an area of more than 400 acres. Starting with only the Department of Sanskrit, it has grown into a multi-factory university as one of the premier centers of advanced study and reaserch in the region.
Our Vision
Be globally acknowledged as a distinguished centre of academic excellence.
Our Missions
To prepare a class of proficient scholars and professionals with ingrained human values and commitment to expand the frontiers of knowledge for the advancement of society.
